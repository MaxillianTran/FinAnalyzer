# Privacy Policy – Max GPT Stock Analyzer

This Custom GPT integrates with Yahoo Finance via RapidAPI to retrieve stock market data only after the user initiates those requests.

No personal information is stored or shared by the GPT author. Any financial data requests are processed through third-party services including:
- OpenAI (text processing)
- RapidAPI + Yahoo Finance (market data retrieval)

Users should review the privacy policies of these third-party providers:
• OpenAI: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy  
• RapidAPI: https://rapidapi.com/privacy
