# Privacy Policy - FinAnalyzer

**Last Updated**: December 2025

## Overview
FinAnalyzer is a Custom GPT designed to help users analyze stock market data. This privacy policy explains how data is handled when you use this service.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What We DO NOT Collect or Store
- **No Personal Information**: The GPT author does not collect, store, or share any personal information about users
- **No Query History**: User questions and requests are not saved or logged by the GPT author
- **No User Profiles**: No user accounts, profiles, or tracking mechanisms are maintained
- **No Financial Data Storage**: Market data retrieved is not stored permanently

### User-Initiated Requests Only
- Financial data is retrieved from Yahoo Finance **ONLY** when you explicitly request it
- The GPT does not proactively fetch or collect data without your explicit instruction
- All data retrieval is transparent and communicated to you before execution

## Third-Party Services

When you use FinAnalyzer, your requests are processed through the following third-party services:

### 1. OpenAI
- **Purpose**: Text processing and conversation management
- **Data Handling**: Subject to [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy)
- **What They Process**: Your messages and conversation context

### 2. RapidAPI + Yahoo Finance
- **Purpose**: Market data retrieval and financial information
- **Data Handling**: Subject to [RapidAPI's Privacy Policy](https://rapidapi.com/privacy) and [Yahoo Finance Terms](https://legal.yahoo.com/us/en/yahoo/privacy/index.html)
- **What They Process**: Stock ticker symbols and data queries you request

## Data Flow
1. You submit a request for stock market data
2. Your request is processed by OpenAI's GPT platform
3. If market data is needed, a request is sent to Yahoo Finance via RapidAPI
4. The data is returned and presented to you
5. No data is permanently stored by the GPT author

## Your Data Rights
- You control what data is requested through your queries
- You can stop using the service at any time
- The GPT author has no data about you to delete or modify

## Security
- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- API keys are securely managed through OpenAI's Actions platform
- No sensitive information is exposed to end users

## Children's Privacy
This service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Disclaimer
This GPT provides informational content only and is not a licensed financial advisor. Market data is provided for informational purposes and should not be considered financial advice.

## Changes to This Policy
Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date.
